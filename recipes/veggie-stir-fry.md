# Veggie Stir Fry

A quick and healthy stir fry packed with colorful vegetables and served over rice.

## Recipe Details

- **Category**: Dinner
- **Cuisine**: Asian
- **Servings**: 4
- **Prep Time**: 10 minutes
- **Cook Time**: 15 minutes
- **Total Time**: 25 minutes
- **Protein**: Tofu
- **Method**: Stir-fried

## Ingredients

- 1 block firm tofu, cubed
- 2 cups mixed vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, snap peas, carrots)
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp fresh ginger, grated
- 3 tbsp gluten-free soy sauce
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 2 cups cooked rice
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 1 tsp cornstarch (optional, for thickening)
- Green onions for garnish

## Instructions

1. Press tofu to remove excess water, then cut into cubes
2. Heat vegetable oil in a large wok or skillet over high heat
3. Add tofu and cook until golden on all sides (5-7 minutes)
4. Remove tofu and set aside
5. Add sesame oil, garlic, and ginger to the pan
6. Add vegetables and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until crisp-tender
7. Return tofu to pan
8. Add soy sauce and toss to combine
9. If using cornstarch, mix with 1 tbsp water and add to thicken sauce
10. Serve over rice and garnish with green onions
