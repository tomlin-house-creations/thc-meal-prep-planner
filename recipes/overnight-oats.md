# Overnight Oats

Easy no-cook breakfast that you prepare the night before. Gluten-free and customizable.

## Recipe Details

- **Category**: Breakfast
- **Cuisine**: American
- **Servings**: 2
- **Prep Time**: 5 minutes
- **Cook Time**: 0 minutes
- **Total Time**: 5 minutes (+ overnight refrigeration)
- **Protein**: Dairy
- **Method**: No-cook

## Ingredients

- 1 cup gluten-free rolled oats
- 1 cup milk (dairy or plant-based)
- 1/2 cup Greek yogurt (optional)
- 1 tbsp chia seeds
- 1 tbsp honey or maple syrup
- 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
- Pinch of salt

### Optional Toppings
- Fresh berries
- Sliced banana
- Pumpkin seeds (nut-free)
- Cinnamon
- Shredded coconut

## Instructions

1. In a jar or container, combine oats, milk, yogurt, chia seeds, honey, vanilla, and salt
2. Stir well to combine
3. Cover and refrigerate overnight (or at least 4 hours)
4. In the morning, stir and add your favorite toppings
5. Enjoy cold or heat in microwave for 30-60 seconds if desired
