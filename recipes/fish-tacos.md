# Fish Tacos

Light and flavorful fish tacos with cabbage slaw and lime crema on gluten-free tortillas.

## Recipe Details

- **Category**: Dinner
- **Cuisine**: Mexican
- **Servings**: 4
- **Prep Time**: 15 minutes
- **Cook Time**: 10 minutes
- **Total Time**: 25 minutes
- **Protein**: Fish
- **Method**: Pan-fried

## Ingredients

### For the Fish
- 1 lb white fish fillets (tilapia, cod, or mahi-mahi)
- 2 tsp chili powder
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1/2 tsp paprika
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 2 tbsp olive oil

### For the Slaw
- 2 cups shredded cabbage
- 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
- Juice of 1 lime
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt to taste

### For Assembly
- 8 gluten-free corn tortillas
- 1 avocado, sliced
- Lime wedges
- Hot sauce (optional)

## Instructions

1. Mix chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper in a small bowl
2. Pat fish dry and season both sides with spice mixture
3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat
4. Cook fish for 3-4 minutes per side until flaky and cooked through
5. While fish cooks, make slaw by combining cabbage, cilantro, lime juice, olive oil, and salt
6. Break fish into chunks with a fork
7. Warm tortillas in a dry pan or microwave
8. Assemble tacos with fish, slaw, and avocado slices
9. Serve with lime wedges and hot sauce
