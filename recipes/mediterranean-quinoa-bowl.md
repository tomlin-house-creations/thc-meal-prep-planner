# Mediterranean Quinoa Bowl

A nutritious and colorful bowl with quinoa, roasted vegetables, and tahini dressing.

## Recipe Details

- **Category**: Lunch
- **Cuisine**: Mediterranean
- **Servings**: 4
- **Prep Time**: 15 minutes
- **Cook Time**: 25 minutes
- **Total Time**: 40 minutes
- **Protein**: Beans
- **Method**: Baked

## Ingredients

### For the Bowl
- 1 cup quinoa
- 2 cups vegetable broth
- 2 cups chickpeas, drained and rinsed
- 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
- 1 zucchini, diced
- 1 red bell pepper, diced
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp oregano
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- Salt and pepper to taste

### For the Tahini Dressing
- 3 tbsp tahini
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- 2 tbsp water
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- Salt to taste

### For Serving
- 2 cups fresh spinach
- 1/4 cup olives, sliced
- Fresh parsley

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C)
2. Rinse quinoa and cook according to package directions using vegetable broth
3. Toss chickpeas, tomatoes, zucchini, and bell pepper with olive oil, oregano, garlic powder, salt, and pepper
4. Spread vegetables on a baking sheet and roast for 20-25 minutes until tender
5. Make tahini dressing by whisking together tahini, lemon juice, water, garlic, and salt
6. Divide spinach among bowls
7. Top with cooked quinoa, roasted vegetables, and olives
8. Drizzle with tahini dressing and garnish with parsley
