# Grilled Chicken Salad

A fresh and filling salad with grilled chicken, mixed greens, and homemade vinaigrette.

## Recipe Details

- **Category**: Lunch
- **Cuisine**: American
- **Servings**: 4
- **Prep Time**: 15 minutes
- **Cook Time**: 12 minutes
- **Total Time**: 27 minutes
- **Protein**: Chicken
- **Method**: Grilled

## Ingredients

### For the Chicken
- 2 large chicken breasts
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp paprika
- Salt and pepper to taste

### For the Salad
- 8 cups mixed greens (lettuce, spinach, arugula)
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- 1 cucumber, sliced
- 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
- 1/4 cup sunflower seeds (nut-free)

### For the Vinaigrette
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
- 1 tsp Dijon mustard
- 1 tsp honey
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Season chicken breasts with olive oil, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper
2. Preheat grill or grill pan to medium-high heat
3. Grill chicken for 6 minutes per side until cooked through (internal temp 165Â°F)
4. Let chicken rest for 5 minutes, then slice
5. Meanwhile, prepare the salad by combining greens, tomatoes, cucumber, and onion in a large bowl
6. Make vinaigrette by whisking together olive oil, vinegar, mustard, honey, salt, and pepper
7. Divide salad among plates, top with sliced chicken and sunflower seeds
8. Drizzle with vinaigrette and serve
